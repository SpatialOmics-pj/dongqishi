## Qishi Dong

Qishi Dong is an Assistant Professor at Shenzhen Technology University (College of Big Data and Internet). He received his Ph.D. in Statistics from Hong Kong Baptist University in 2023. His research interests include probabilistic models, approximate Bayesian inference, and their applications in single-cell and spatial omics. He serves as a reviewer for CSDA / AISTAT / AAAI. He has worked or visited Huawei (Hong Kong) Noah’s Ark Lab, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen), and The University of Hong Kong.  
Contact: dongqishi@sztu.edu.cn
